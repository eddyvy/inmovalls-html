Property Management

Since 1963, we have been dedicated to managing and administering estates, communities, and properties.

Throughout all these years, we have achieved our goal: the perfect functioning of communities, their conservation, maintenance, and repair of general services and facilities.

For this reason, countless communities and individuals have placed their trust in us, thanks to the professionalism and seriousness of our staff and collaborators.

We continuously improve the quality of the services your community or property receives, monitoring the work of providers, ensuring compliance with deadlines and budgets, and evaluating the satisfaction level of the owners. We provide clear, simple, and up-to-date information on the economic status of your community or property and the operation of its services. We optimize for the benefit of the property, with professional, fair, and legally compliant criteria.

We maintain properties for their proper conservation and compliance with safety, hygiene, and operational standards in the interest of their residents. For this purpose, we have a 24-hour maintenance service, every day of the year.

We have a series of departments that are responsible for providing our clients with comprehensive advice and management of their services:

  • Acquisition and legalization of the minutes book in the Property Registry.
  • Obtaining the community's tax identification number (CIF).
  • Opening a bank account with the best market conditions.
  • Drafting of the Statutes and Internal Rules.
  • Comprehensive preparation of receipts.
  • Obtaining quotes for works and repairs.
  • Monitoring and control of the works and providers.
  • Drafting and sending to all owners, the minutes of the meeting and transcription to the minutes book.
  • Continuous information to owners on matters affecting them.
